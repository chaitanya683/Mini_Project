About Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC)

IRCTC is a public sector enterprise under the Ministry of Railways, Government of India. Established in 1997, it is responsible for providing catering services to passengers traveling on Indian Railways trains. Over the years, IRCTC has expanded its services to include online ticket booking, packaged tours, and other railway-related hospitality services.

IRCTC's mission is to:

  • Provide high-quality catering services to railway passengers.
  • Facilitate online ticket booking for passengers traveling on Indian Railways trains.
  • Offer a wide range of travel and tourism services to its customers.
  • Contribute to the growth of the Indian Railways.

IRCTC has a strong presence across India with a network of catering units, reservation counters, and sales offices. It constantly strives to improve its services and offer its customers a better travel experience.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of catering, tourism, and related services in the Indian Railways sector, and to be a global player in the hospitality industry.
